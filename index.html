About me

I grew up in the foothills of Idaho learning as much as I could about music. After high school, I was fortune enough to attend Berklee College of Music, where I further honed my skills and expanded my knowledge. After a couple of years of living in Los Angeles and working in the music industry, I discovered a passion for coding.

There's a story about IBM in the 70s. They told their recruiters to seek out musicians if they ran out of programmers. They did this because musicians have a number of qualities that lend themselves to the development of software and new technologies. Much like reading a score, we have to have an attention to detail while keeping the big picture in view, and we can work with others while serving the needs of a whole project.
